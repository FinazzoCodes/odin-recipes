Quick and easy Chicken Quesadillas

Prepare to indulge in the mouthwatering flavors of our herb-seasoned roasted chicken quesadillas. Shredded herb-seasoned roasted chicken takes center stage, alongside a medley of diced tomatoes, black beans, and sweet kernel corn, all wrapped up in soft flour tortillas. A perfect blend of taco seasoning adds a burst of zesty goodness. Top it all off with a generous helping of shredded cheddar cheese, creating a gooey, melty masterpiece. A touch of oil in the pan ensures a crisp, golden finish that will leave you craving seconds. Get ready for a taste sensation that's sure to become a family favorite

Steps

  • 1 Herb Seasoned Roasted Chicken shredded
  • 6 flour tortillas
  • 1 large tomato diced
  • 1 can black beans drained
  • 1 can sweet kernel corn drained
  • 2 tablespoons taco seasoning
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • Oil

Ingredients

  1. In a large bowl, combine the diced tomatoes, black beans, corn, and taco seasoning. Set aside.
  2. Heat a large skillet to med-high heat and add Â½ teaspoon oil and a tortilla. Top tortilla with a handful of shredded cheese, about 1/3 cup of the vegetable mixture, and a serving of shredded chicken.
  3. Fold the tortilla and cook on both sides until crispy and golden. Remove from the pan and repeat with remaining tortillas. Then enjoy your quick and easy chicken quesadillas!
